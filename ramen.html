    • Step by step cooking ramen

    1. Boil the eggs and set them aside.
    2. In a large pot, bring water to a boil and cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
    3. In a separate pot, heat the chicken broth until it comes to a simmer.
    4. Add the soy sauce, miso paste, and sesame oil to the broth. Stir until well combined.
    5. Slice the cooked chicken breast and set aside.
    6. Blanch the bok choy in boiling water for 1 minute, then transfer to an ice bath to cool. Drain and set aside.
    7. Divide the cooked ramen noodles into bowls.
    8. Pour the hot broth over the noodles.
    9. Top with sliced chicken, blanched bok choy, sliced green onions, and halved boiled eggs.
    10. Serve hot and enjoy!
